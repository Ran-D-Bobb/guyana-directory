## Overview

| **Users** | Discover quality, verified businesses |
| **Platform** | Sustainable income stream |

## Eligibility Criteria

### Why Have Criteria?

> "Not every business should be featured. Quality control protects your platform's reputation and ensures users trust featured recommendations."

### Required Before a Business Can Pay for Featured Status

| Requirement | Why It Matters |
|-------------|----------------|
| **Complete Profile (100%)** | Ensures professional presentation |
| **Minimum 2-3 Photos** | Visual appeal drives engagement |
| **Verified Phone Number** | Proves business is legitimate |
| **Verified Email Address** | Communication channel confirmed |
| **Active for 14-30 Days** | Filters out temporary/fake listings |
| **No Unresolved Complaints** | Quality control measure |

### Optional Premium Criteria

| Requirement | Benefit |
|-------------|---------|
| **3.5+ Star Rating** | Rewards quality businesses |
| **5+ Reviews** | Social proof established |
| **Response Rate >80%** | Active business owner |

### Criteria Benefits

```
For the Platform:
├── Maintains quality standards
├── Builds user trust
├── Creates aspirational goal for free-tier businesses
└── Justifies premium pricing

For Businesses:
├── Featured status feels earned, not just bought
├── Differentiates from competitors
└── Badge of quality
```

## Pricing Strategy

### Market Considerations for Guyana

| Factor | Implication |
|--------|-------------|
| GDP Per Capita | Price must be accessible |
| Business Sizes Vary | Need tiered options |
| Cash Flow Patterns | Monthly payments preferred |
| Competition | Undercut traditional advertising |

### Recommended Pricing

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                     MONTHLY PRICING                              │
├──────────────┬──────────────────┬──────────────────┬────────────┤
│    Tier      │   GYD (Local)    │   USD (Approx)   │  Per Day   │
├──────────────┼──────────────────┼──────────────────┼────────────┤
│ Highlighted  │  GYD 5,000-10,000│     $25-50       │  ~$1/day   │
├──────────────┼──────────────────┼──────────────────┼────────────┤
│ Featured     │ GYD 15,000-25,000│     $75-125      │  ~$3/day   │
├──────────────┼──────────────────┼──────────────────┼────────────┤
│ Spotlight    │ GYD 40,000-60,000│    $200-300      │  ~$8/day   │

### Pricing Psychology

```
"For less than a newspaper ad, reach thousands of
active customers searching for exactly what you offer."

Highlighted: "Less than GYD 350/day"
Featured:    "Less than GYD 850/day"
Spotlight:   "Less than GYD 2,000/day"

### Value Proposition by Tier

| Tier | Best For | ROI Argument |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Highlighted** | "I need a little boost" | "One extra customer covers the monthly cost" |
| **Featured** | "I want real visibility" | "5-10 new customers/month = 10x ROI" |

### Ad Types Explained

#### 1. Banner Ads

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Placement** | Top of page, between sections, footer |
| **Format** | Image (728x90, 320x50 mobile) |
| **Pricing Model** | Flat monthly fee OR CPM (cost per 1000 views) |
| **Best For** | Brand awareness, promotions, events |

**Suggested Pricing:**
- Top Banner: GYD 8,000-15,000/week ($40-75)
- Mid Banner: GYD 5,000-10,000/week ($25-50)
- Footer Banner: GYD 3,000-6,000/week ($15-30)

#### 2. Sponsored Listings

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Placement** | Top of search results, category pages |
| **Format** | Same as regular listing + "Sponsored" tag |
| **Pricing Model** | Pay-per-click OR flat monthly |
| **Best For** | Direct customer acquisition |

**Suggested Pricing:**
- Per Click: GYD 100-200 ($0.50-1.00)
- Monthly Flat: GYD 10,000-20,000 ($50-100)

#### 3. Category Sponsorship (Premium)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Placement** | Exclusive top spot in a category |
| **Format** | Large featured card + "Official Sponsor" badge |
| **Pricing Model** | Monthly exclusive contract |
| **Best For** | Market leaders, brand dominance |

**Suggested Pricing:**
- GYD 50,000-100,000/month ($250-500)
- **Only 1 sponsor per category** (creates scarcity)

#### 4. Push Notification Ads (Future)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Placement** | User's device notification |
| **Format** | Short message with business name |
| **Pricing Model** | Per notification sent |
| **Best For** | Time-sensitive promotions |

**Suggested Pricing:**
- GYD 5,000-10,000 per blast ($25-50)
- Limited to 1-2 per week to avoid user annoyance

## Key Success Factors

### Do's

| Action | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| **Start with manual sales** | Personal outreach closes deals |
| **Offer free trials** | Let businesses see value first |
| **Show clear ROI** | "You got 50 views this week" |
| **Create scarcity** | "Only 5 Spotlight spots available" |
| **Make upgrading easy** | One-click tier upgrades |
| **Provide excellent support** | Featured businesses are VIPs |

### Don'ts

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| **Too many featured businesses** | Dilutes the value |
| **No eligibility criteria** | Low-quality businesses hurt reputation |
| **Hidden pricing** | Builds distrust |
| **Ignoring mobile** | Most users are on mobile |
| **No analytics** | Businesses need to see ROI |

## Summary: Your Options

### Option A: Start Simple

- Launch with **Featured tier only**
- Manual approval and payment
- Add more tiers later based on demand

**Pros:** Fast to implement, learn what businesses want
**Cons:** Limited revenue potential initially

### Option B: Full Tier System

- Launch with all **3 tiers**
- Self-service signup and payment
- Automated renewals

**Pros:** Maximum revenue potential, professional
**Cons:** More development time, need payment integration

### Option C: Advertising First

- Start with **banner ads only**
- Sell to larger businesses manually
- Add featured listings later

**Pros:** Simpler technically, immediate revenue
**Cons:** Limited audience (only big businesses)

### Recommended: Option A → B

1. **Month 1:** Launch Featured tier with manual process
2. **Month 2-3:** Add Highlighted and Spotlight tiers
3. **Month 4+:** Add self-service payment and advertising
